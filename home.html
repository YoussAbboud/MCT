Mission Statement

We strive to meet and exceed our customers' expectations. Our aim is to execute each job that we handle as our first transaction with a specific client, and in which we are supposed to demonstrate a professional execution and a strong after-sales support.

Our Objective

Our objective is To become a leading electromechanical solution provider for our customers from the various sectors that we serve; to name few: power industry, Infrastructure and residential, water, oil and gas and the IT infrastructure.

About Us

MCT OMAN

Muscat Capital Trading (MCT Oman) has been formed by a group of experienced engineers coming from various backgrounds and industries. Since its creation in 2012 as IT solutions company we have evolved multiple times to expand our business segments and industries. The last transformation in 2018 has seen an expansion of our product portfolio to provide electromechanical products to our customers. As a local trading company, we have strong links with overseas manufacturers and a local representative for one of the very famous MEP solution providers in Europe.
